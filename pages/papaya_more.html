Name & Image

Plant Name: Papaya

Scientific Name: Carica papaya

Family: Caricaceae

Type: Fruit plant

2. Basic Info

Papaya is a tropical fruit plant known for its nutritious fruits rich in vitamins A and C. It grows fast and is cultivated widely in warm regions.

3. Common Diseases

  • Papaya Ring Spot Virus
  • Powdery Mildew
  • Anthochnose

4. Pesticides

  • Neem oil sprays
  • Copper fungicides
  • Insecticides for aphid control

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Plant resistant varieties.
  • Destroy virus-infected plants immediately.
  • Spray neem oil regularly.
  • Ensure proper irrigation and drainage.
