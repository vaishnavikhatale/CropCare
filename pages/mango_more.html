Name & Image

Plant Name: Mango

Scientific Name: Mangifera indica

Family: Anacardiaceae

Type: Fruit tree

2. Basic Info

Mango is a tropical fruit tree known as the "King of Fruits". It thrives in warm climates, requires deep soil, and produces sweet and juicy fruits rich in Vitamin A & C.

3. Common Diseases

  • Powdery Mildew
  • Anthracnose
  • Sooty Mold
  • Bacterial Canker

4. Pesticides

  • Copper oxychloride
  • Sulfur dust (for powdery mildew)
  • Neem oil sprays

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Prune infected branches.
  • Spray fungicides during flowering.
  • Maintain orchard hygiene.
  • Ensure good airflow around the tree.
