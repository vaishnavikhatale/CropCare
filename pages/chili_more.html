Name & Image

Plant Name: Chili

Scientific Name: Capsicum annuum

Family: Solanaceae

Type: Vegetable/Spice crop

2. Basic Info

Chili is cultivated for its spicy fruits used as a vegetable and spice. It requires warm climates and well-drained soil.

3. Common Diseases

  • Anthracnose
  • Powdery Mildew
  • Leaf Curl Virus

4. Pesticides

  • Copper fungicide
  • Neem oil spray
  • Imidacloprid (for leaf curl vectors)

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Use resistant varieties.
  • Destroy infected plants early.
  • Spray neem oil regularly.
  • Control insect vectors.
