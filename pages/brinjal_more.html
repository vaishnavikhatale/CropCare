Plant Name: Brinjal (Eggplant)

Scientific Name: Solanum melongena

2. Basic Info

Brinjal is a widely grown vegetable known for its purple edible fruit. It grows best in warm climates with well-drained fertile soil.

3. Common Diseases

  • Wilt disease
  • Fruit borer attack
  • Leaf spot
  • Root-knot nematodes

4. Pesticides

  • Neem oil sprays
  • Bacillus thuringiensis (Bt) for fruit borer
  • Copper fungicide
