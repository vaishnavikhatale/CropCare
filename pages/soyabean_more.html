Name & Image

Plant Name: Soybean

Scientific Name: Glycine max

Family: Fabaceae

Type: Legume crop

2. Basic Info

Soybean is a major legume crop used for oil, protein, and livestock feed. It improves soil fertility through nitrogen fixation.

3. Common Diseases

  • Rust disease
  • Root Rot
  • Bacterial Blight
  • Downy Mildew

4. Pesticides

  • Copper fungicides
  • Neem oil
  • Triazole fungicides

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Use disease-resistant varieties.
  • Rotate with non-legume crops.
  • Apply fungicides during early infection.
  • Maintain field hygiene.
