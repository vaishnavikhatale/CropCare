Name & Image

Plant Name: Peas

Scientific Name: Pisum sativum

Family: Fabaceae

Type: Legume vegetable

2. Basic Info

Peas are cool-season legumes grown for their edible seeds. They are rich in protein, fiber, and vitamins and help improve soil fertility by fixing nitrogen.

3. Common Diseases

  • Powdery Mildew
  • Root Rot
  • Downy Mildew
  • Fusarium Wilt

4. Pesticides

  • Sulfur dust
  • Copper fungicide
  • Neem oil

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Practice crop rotation.
  • Ensure proper drainage.
  • Apply fungicides as preventive measures.
  • Use disease-resistant varieties.
