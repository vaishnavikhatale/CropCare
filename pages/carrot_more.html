Name & Image

Plant Name: Carrot

Scientific Name: Daucus carota

Family: Apiaceae

Type: Root vegetable

2. Basic Info

Carrot is a root vegetable known for its orange edible root rich in beta-carotene. It grows best in sandy loam soil with good drainage.

3. Common Diseases

  • Alternaria Leaf Blight
  • Powdery Mildew
  • Root Knot Nematodes

4. Pesticides

  • Copper fungicide
  • Neem oil spray
  • Carbofuran (for nematodes)

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Rotate crops regularly.
  • Maintain soil health.
  • Use bio-pesticides where possible.
  • Remove infected leaves early.
