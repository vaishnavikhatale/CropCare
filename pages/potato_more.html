Name & Image

Plant Name: Potato

Scientific Name: Solanum tuberosum

Family: Solanaceae

Type: Root vegetable crop

2. Basic Info

Potato is an underground tuber crop widely cultivated for food. It is rich in carbohydrates and used worldwide in various dishes.

3. Common Diseases

  • Late Blight
  • Early Blight
  • Black Scurf

4. Pesticides

  • Mancozeb (for blight)
  • Copper oxychloride
  • Neem-based sprays

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Plant disease-free seed tubers.
  • Practice crop rotation.
  • Remove infected plants quickly.
  • Use fungicide sprays during wet season.
