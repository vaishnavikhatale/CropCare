Name & Image

Fruit Name: Guava

Scientific Name: Psidium guajava

Family: Myrtaceae

Type: Fruit tree

2. Basic Info

Guava is a tropical fruit tree producing sweet and tangy fruits. It is rich in Vitamin C and grows in diverse soils with little care.

3. Common Diseases

  • Wilt Disease
  • Anthracnose
  • Fruit Fly Infestation

4. Pesticides

  • Copper fungicide
  • Neem oil spray
  • Chlorpyrifos (for fruit flies)

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Maintain orchard hygiene.
  • Spray fungicides during flowering.
  • Use fruit fly traps.
  • Apply neem oil regularly.
