3. Common Diseases

  • Early Blight
  • Late Blight
  • Leaf Curl Virus
  • Powdery Mildew

4. Pesticides

  • Copper-based fungicides (for blight)
  • Neem oil (organic control)
  • Sulfur spray (for powdery mildew)

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Rotate crops regularly to prevent soil-borne diseases.
  • Use disease-resistant tomato varieties.
  • Ensure proper spacing for airflow between plants.
  • Apply organic pesticides like neem oil when needed.
  • Remove infected leaves immediately to stop spread.
