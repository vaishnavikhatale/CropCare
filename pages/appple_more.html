Name & Image

Fruit Name: Apple

Scientific Name: Malus domestica

Family: Rosaceae

Type: Fruit tree

2. Basic Info

Apple is a temperate fruit crop that grows in cool climates. Apples are rich in fiber, vitamins, and antioxidants and are cultivated worldwide.

3. Common Diseases

  • Apple Scab
  • Powdery Mildew
  • Fire Blight
  • Cedar Apple Rust

4. Pesticides

  • Copper fungicides
  • Sulfur sprays
  • Neem oil (organic)

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Prune trees for airflow.
  • Apply fungicides at early growth stages.
  • Use resistant varieties.
  • Maintain orchard cleanliness.
