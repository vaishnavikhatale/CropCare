Name & Image

Fruit Name: Banana

Scientific Name: Musa paradisiaca

Family: Musaceae

Type: Fruit plant

2. Basic Info

Banana is one of the most important fruit crops in the tropics. It grows in humid climates and provides fruits rich in potassium and fiber.

3. Common Diseases

  • Panama Wilt
  • Sigatoka Leaf Spot
  • Bunchy Top Virus

4. Pesticides

  • Carbendazim (for wilt)
  • Copper fungicides
  • Neem oil (organic control)

5. How to Solve Issues

  • Use disease-free suckers for planting.
  • Maintain proper field sanitation.
  • Improve drainage in waterlogged areas.
  • Apply fungicides when necessary.
